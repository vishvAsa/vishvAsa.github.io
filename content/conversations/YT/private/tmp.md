“netare tu kadācana” these words make it clear that the anAthAdis are never fit for pUjana, which contradicts the matsyapurANavAkya that I shared.

Also, in the pancaviMsha, there is a vAkya that expressly calls for the giving of cows as dakSiNA (since this is dakSiNA and not dAna, one may equate this with pUjana as well?) to an unlearned person who is nonetheless ArSeya as well as one who is both learned and ArSeya. One may argue that this serves as a lingapramANa for the pUrtakarma to be done in favour of unlearned and poor brAhmaNas: see 20.15.9-11




Therefore, unless you are willing to take the position that it is never acceptable to do pUjana or dAna for the anAthAdis at any time, the above shlokas will not hold. Since such a position—at least in the limited case of anAthAdi brAhmaNas—is contradicted by both shruti and smRti, the position on mAtsaryAtmaka hetu still stands.
