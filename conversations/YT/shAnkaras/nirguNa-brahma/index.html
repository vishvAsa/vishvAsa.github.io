विस्तारः (द्रष्टुं नोद्यम्)

Challenge statement - Show any sentence from shAnkara bhAShya where “parameshvara”, by itself, unambiguously refers directly to nirguNa brahman (SB) without going via saguNa brahman (NB). (“निरस्तसर्वविशेषं पारमेश्वरं” obviously doesn’t cut it - it explicitly deletes visheSha from parameshvara.)

shrI Vikram Jagannathan’s mail - https://lists.advaita-vedanta.org/archives/advaita-l/2025-November/064339.html
